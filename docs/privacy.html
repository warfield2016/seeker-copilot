Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

What We Collect

Seeker Copilot reads your public on-chain data through Seed Vault to display portfolio information. We collect:

  • Your public wallet address (to fetch on-chain balances)
  • Portfolio queries sent to our analysis engine
  • Basic usage analytics (app opens, feature usage)

What We Don't Collect

  • Private keys or seed phrases — never, under any circumstances
  • Personal identity information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or device data

How We Use Your Data

Wallet addresses and portfolio data are used solely to generate portfolio analysis, risk scores, and trade signals. Queries are processed by our backend analysis engine and are not stored permanently.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to provide functionality:

  • Solana RPC nodes — to read on-chain data
  • Jupiter Price API — to fetch token prices
  • Groq — for natural language analysis processing

Each service has its own privacy policy. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Security

All API communications use HTTPS encryption. Wallet interactions happen through Seed Vault's secure enclave. We do not store private keys or have custody of any funds.

Your Rights

You can disconnect your wallet at any time to stop all data access. No account creation is required. We do not retain portfolio data after your session ends.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
