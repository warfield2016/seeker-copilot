Why This Exists

150,000 Seeker phones shipped. Hundreds of thousands of people holding tokens, staking SOL, providing liquidity on Jupiter and Drift and Kamino. And not a single app on the device that helps them understand what they actually own.

I built this because I needed it myself. I was checking three different websites to understand my own portfolio. Copying wallet addresses between tabs. Doing mental math on concentration risk. Wondering if the protocol I was lending on had ever been audited.

That's a broken experience. The Seeker phone has Seed Vault — the most secure way to interact with Solana — but there's nothing sitting on top of it that actually helps you think about your positions.

Seeker Copilot fixes that. It reads your on-chain positions through Seed Vault, runs the numbers, and gives you a clear picture: what you hold, what's at risk, what's trending, and what the protocols you're trusting actually look like from a security standpoint.

What It Does

Portfolio View

All your tokens, DeFi positions, staked SOL, and LP positions in one screen. Real-time USD values. 24h changes. No tab switching.

Risk Scoring

Quantitative risk breakdown: concentration, volatility exposure, impermanent loss, liquidation proximity. Numbers, not vibes.

Protocol Safety

Every DeFi protocol in your portfolio gets a safety score. Audit status, incident history, maturity. Know what you're trusting.

Market Signals

Trending narratives, momentum shifts, and opportunities you're missing. Positioned for what's happening now, not last month.

Trade Signals

Actionable recommendations with confidence scores. Rebalance suggestions grounded in your actual risk profile, not generic advice.

Seed Vault Native

Built on Mobile Wallet Adapter. Your keys never leave the secure enclave. View-only portfolio reading. Zero custody risk.

The Vision

The Solana ecosystem has world-class infrastructure but a gap in intelligence. Jupiter aggregates swaps. Drift runs perps. Kamino optimizes yields. But nobody is helping the human at the end of the chain make sense of it all.

I want Seeker Copilot to be the layer that sits between you and the complexity. Not a trading bot. Not a yield chaser. A tool that helps you see clearly — so you can make decisions with your own brain, armed with the right data.

The long-term goal is an intelligence layer for every Seeker user — from the DeFi power user in Lagos running a five-figure portfolio across eight protocols, to someone in Ho Chi Minh City who just bought their first SOL and doesn't know what staking means yet.

Both of them deserve to understand what they own.
